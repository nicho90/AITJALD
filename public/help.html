Use our web application is to visualize open data of the city of Münster.

  • Purpose of this application

    The purpose of this web application is to visualize open data of the city of Münster.

    • What you can do in this application

      Displaying Map with different features and options
    • Three categories example city, district, citydistrict

      Click the categories to select city, district and citydistrict

  • Compare Cities

    Click the categories to select the compare cities and you can see blue color when it is clicked
    In this application there is only one city. So, it give Muenster population data

    • Compare Districts

      Click the categories to select the compare districts and you can see blue color when it is clicked
      In this application there is districts of Muenster.When you click this option you get the clicked districts population data with compare to another clicked district population data

    • Compare Citydistricts

      Click the categories to select the compare Citydistricts and you can see blue color when it is clicked
      In this application there is Citydistricts of Muenster.When you click this option you get the clicked Citydistricts population data with compare to another clicked Citydistrict population data

    • Layers

      Click the Layers to select the different layers of map


    • Slider

      Slide the slider to select the different years of population data which is reflected on map by changing colors


  • Main population

    Chossing main population gives you a chart of population data who live in the main place of the citydistricts, districts, and Muenster city


    Here in this snapshot, I choose the district west of the city Muenster to display a chart of the main population data in different years


    • Population entitled to live in

      Chossing Population entitled to live in gives you a chart of population data who are entitled to live in the citydistricts, districts of Muenster city


      Here in this snapshot, I choose the district nord of the city Muenster to display a chart of the population entitled to live there data in different years


    • Male population by age

      Chossing male population by age gives you a chart of male population data by different age group of the citydistricts, districts of Muenster city


      Here in this snapshot, I choose the district nord of the city Muenster to display a chart of the male population data by different age group in the year 2014


    • Female population by age

      Chossing female population by age gives you a chart of female population data by different age group of the citydistricts, districts of Muenster city


      Here in this snapshot, I choose the district nord of the city Muenster to display a chart of the female population data by different age group in the year 2014


    • Population by age

      Chossing Population by age gives you a chart of population data by different agegroup of the citydistricts, districts of Muenster city in the year 2014


    • General

      It provides a general information related to the districts, citydistricts and Muenster city
